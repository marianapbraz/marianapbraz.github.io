Ateliê

App Design (UI/UX), Communication Plan

The Ateliê project was born of the need for artisans to have an online space to publish and publicize their art. The idea, which took shape and color through the design of the app (Adobe XD), includes the Ateliê Brand Identity Manual, a Media Kit, a duly scheduled Communication Plan and other content that you can discover below.
